millionaire's bacon

ingredients

    millionaire's rub
  • 4 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons everything bagel seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons course-ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • --
  • 1 package of thick cut bacon
lay strips of bacon across a baking sheet covered in foil. in a bowl, mix all rub ingredients together then liberally sprinkle both sides of each bacon slice. put bacon pan into cold oven, then turn on to 400¬∞. do not open oven while preheating! once oven is preheated to set temperature, bake bacon for 15-20 minutes more, or until nice and brown. let bacon cool on cooling rack, serve and enjoy!
